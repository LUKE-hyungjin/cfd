Software and learning resources for CFD simulation.

- SimVascular - Cardiovascular CFD specialized
- OpenFOAM - General purpose CFD

## Commercial

- ANSYS Fluent/CFX
- COMSOL Multiphysics

## Medical Imaging

- 3D Slicer
- ITK-SNAP

## Recommended Textbooks

- Versteeg & Malalasekera, "An Introduction to CFD"
- Fung, "Biomechanics: Circulation"
- Chandran, "Biofluid Mechanics"
